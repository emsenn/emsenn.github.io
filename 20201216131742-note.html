note

A note is a type of information, consisting of, at a minimum, an identifier and content. In many information management tools, a note is the most basic unit of information.

Notes are implemented within Groundhog Emacs, as the most-basic information schema. Notes are also an important part of the Zettelkasten method of note-taking.
