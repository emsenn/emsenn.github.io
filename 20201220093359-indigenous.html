indigenous

To be indigenous can mean two different things, depending on if a person is human or not. For non-humans, to be indigenous means to have evolved or established a generations-long niche in a physical environment. For humans, being indigenous to a thing means to live focused on seeking a balanced relationship with that thing. Most commonly, the term used in that context is to refer to humans being "indigenous to place," (Braiding Sweetgrass) meaning that a person (and their culture) are focused on finding a balanced relationship with the other things in their environment.
