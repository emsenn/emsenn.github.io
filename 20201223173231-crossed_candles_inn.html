Crossed Candles Inn

People here
Lexandra Terr

The Crossed Candles Inn is a relatively simple building: four posts are sunk deep into the silty clay soil of Arathel County, some crossbeams were braced into place, and vertical slats were hammered on over those. Some windows are covered with shutters, and an open doorway faces toward a fork in the Arathel-Ack road.

From outside the Crossed Candles Inn, the Arathel-Ack road continues north toward Arathel County, southeast toward the town of Bellybrush, and southwest toward Ack.

Inside, the Crossed Candles Inn is a simple wood shack, with several shuttered windows. There are a few wood cots in one corner, and a long table which serves as the bar in another. During the day, there are usually a few travelers sitting at the bar, resting as they move across the GAME.
