human

A human is any type of person that's genetically a Homo sapien.

There's some other thoughts about what a human might be, for example Charles Keil, an anthropologist, proposes that there are classes of humans, for example a co-taxonomy of Humo ludens collaborans; see Charles Keil: "dear gretas".
