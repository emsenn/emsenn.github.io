Parts of a daily record
  • Note taken on [2020-12-24 Thu 09:41]
    Started writing this down.

There are many parts to a complete daily record:

Part Implementation Purpose
Title #+title: 2020-12-24 Specifies the day the record covers.
Date #+date: [2020-12-24 Thu 06:34] Specifies the record's initialization time.
Media log * Media log Holds entries about found and/or registered media.
Journal * Journal  

All together, that results in a record that looks something like the following. /(Note, this was constructed manually, not from the above table data; there may be errors in conversion. 

#+title: 2020-12-24
#+begin_head
#+ghe_schema: daily-record-0.1.2
#+author: emsenn
#+email: emsenn@emsenn.net
#+date: [2020-12-24 Thu 06:34]
#+roam_tags: daily-record
#+end_head


* Introduction
This is my (emsenn's) personal record for [2020-12-24 Thu]. If you are viewing this record outside its original environment, please be aware it may be heavily redacted.
* 2020-12-24
** Activity log                                                    :noexport:
** Journal                                                         :noexport:

	(defun ghe-org-table-to-defvar (table)
	  (string-join 
	      (mapcar
	       (lambda (item)
		 (print (concat "(defvar "
				(car item)
				" \""
				(car (cdr item))
				"\" \""
				(car (cdr (cdr item)))
				"\")\n")))
		 table)))
	(defun ghe-org-table-to-setq (table)
	  (string-join 
	      (mapcar
	       (lambda (item)
		 (print (concat "(setq "
				(car item)
				" "
				(car (cdr item))
				")\n")))
		 table)))
