Introduction

North Phoenixrock

Trade

North Phoenixrock uses a gift economy with outsiders. Gifts are made available at the base of the settlement. Here's a list of gifts the settlemnet likes:

Dirt
Since they live on the side of an active volcano, the settlement has no natural dirt.
Ores
Again, living on a volcano, they have no access to metal.

Here's a list of gifts that are frequently abundant for visitors:

Stone
As the settlement is constantly terracing the volcano and expanding into its walls, there is usually a great volume of stone available.
Gravel
Same reason as above.
Hay
The green-spaces of North Phoenixrock are, for now, mostly cultivated for grass for hay, which is used extensively by the settlement as a building material and fuel.
