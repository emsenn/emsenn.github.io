DISCOVERED Andrew Ragland: "Last Geomancer: A Paradox of Hypocrisy"

  • Note taken on [2020-12-10 Thu 10:14]

Behind-the-scenes look at Andrew Ragland's Last Geomancer fantasy serial.

In a lot of conceptions of “post-colonial” negotiations between Indigenous peoples and colonizers and those forced into Collaboration with the colonizers, the Indigenous peoples are portrayed as judge, jury, and executioner. To me, this always comes off as a projection of colonizer’s fears of having their own tendencies to subjugate turned back on them.

In reality, it has always been much more like how Andrew presents here: An exchange of information between two parties as equal, and discussion about what today and tomorrow should look like based on that. And the answers are rarely good, but that doesn’t change that they are the answers.

  • Source: https://jackhollow.substack.com/p/2020-12-10-a-paradox-of-hypocrisy
