  • Note taken on [2020-12-10 Thu 14:51]

REGISTERED Andrew Ragland: "Last Geomancer: A Paradox of Hypocrisy"

  • Note taken on [2020-12-10 Thu 10:14]

Behind-the-scenes look at Andrew Ragland's Last Geomancer fantasy serial.

In a lot of conceptions of “post-colonial” negotiations between Indigenous peoples and colonizers and those forced into Collaboration with the colonizers, the Indigenous peoples are portrayed as judge, jury, and executioner. To me, this always comes off as a projection of colonizer’s fears of having their own tendencies to subjugate turned back on them.

In reality, it has always been much more like how Andrew presents here: An exchange of information between two parties as equal, and discussion about what today and tomorrow should look like based on that. And the answers are rarely good, but that doesn’t change that they are the answers.

  • Source: https://jackhollow.substack.com/p/2020-12-10-a-paradox-of-hypocrisy

REGISTERED The Nation: "Indigenous Cultures Take Root in New York"

:LOGBOOK:

  • Note taken on [2020-12-10 Thu 08:58]

This is an amazing series of photographs and piece of journalism. I, and many Indigenous peers, have been dismissed as “racial supremacists” in the past for our belief that it is Indigenous cultures that are capable of survival, not kyriarchists.

It’s difficult to tolerate such hateful misrepresentation, but made easier by two things: an understanding that the kyriarchists’ whole worldview is a misrepresentation; their view of me is nothing personal. And, regardless of what the kyriachists think or how they act, it doesn’t change that it is Indigenous ways of living are bringing humans into each new day, far safer than the kyriarchists ever have.

Source
https://www.thenation.com/article/society/indigenous-migrants-new-york/
